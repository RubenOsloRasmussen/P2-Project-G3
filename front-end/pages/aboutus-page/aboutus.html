About Us

Welcome to SudokuElo, a website made for Sudoku players who want to improve their skills.

On SudokuElo, your performance on a preset puzzle is turned into a proficiency score from 1 to 10. This score helps us suggest the strategies most relevant for you and gives you new puzzles that match your skill level. Your score is calculated based on both the time you spend on the puzzle and the mistakes you make.

You can pause the timer if you need a break and continue any time. And don’t worry, mistakes aren’t counted per wrong digit. Instead, we count error episodes. An error episode begins when your puzzle goes 'off track' and ends when you eventually correct all the wrong digits.

You can also visit our strategy page where you can read about all of the strategies and see which ones match your current proficiency score.
